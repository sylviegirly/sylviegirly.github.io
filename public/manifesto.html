Manifesto

just wanna find the softest grass i can find and lay down in it and look at the sky for a very very very long time

I've seen these around, and I'm not sure exactly still what to write, but I really liked Bechno Kid's and Petra's manifesto a lot and wanted to write something up in spirit ... I'll probably hammer it out over time and add more, but I've always been a person whose pro "make your own space with all ur 'cringy' loves".

God knows I had too many stamps and decos and everything on my DeviantArt back then. I do have my own personal carrd still, and I love carrd for its flexibility and ease of access to all people alike (especially since I've made templates for it!!), but ... it doesn't hit like a custom website does.

That, and it doesn't really help that Tumblr goes through a potential shutting down crisis almost every year now. I'd really be depressed to lose that site, considering the customization of it (the only last site I know that lets you make themes + where I learned all of my CSS/HTML from), and the fact I've been on it for 13 years of my life (2012 Mishapocalypse babeyyyy). So I guess this site is in a way a love letter to Tumblr as well because it's what has taught me CSS/HTML, or how to pluck it apart and to work with it.

Um, so this site will probably just be a collection of my favourite things, my OCs, and just what makes me ... me. I'm not very good at aggregating things about myself, but I'd like to have this as a website that's just for myself and for people to see who I am. I most likely won't be posting my artwork since I already have dedicated sites for that (and all of my commissions are hosted through VGen), so it'll mostly just be very personal things, especially as someone who's very forgetful for what I even like sometimes.

I'll be refining this page over time as I read more manifestos and properly word out my own thoughts on things.
