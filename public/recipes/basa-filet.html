Introduction

Oven baked basa fish marinated with butter, sweet paprika and dried basil. Fish basa fillets are easy, quick and healthy meal for the party gathering or family dinner.

  • Prep Time: 30min (prep) + 30min (cook)
  • Servings: 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 basa fish fillets
  • 4 tsp. unsalted butter
  • 1/2 Tbsp. sweet paprika
  • 1/2 Tbsp. dried basil
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 1/8 tsp. lemon juice
  • 4 lemon slices

Cooking Instructions

  1. Prepare baking sheet with parchment paper. Put basa fish on the baking sheet.
  2. Melt butter in the small glass bowl. Add sweet paprika, dried basil, salt and lemon juice.
  3. Spread the marinate on both sides of fish using cooking brush or spoon. Marinate for 20 minutes.
  4. Put lemon slices on top of the fish.
  5. Preheat oven to 350 F.
  6. Bake for 30 minutes uncovered.
