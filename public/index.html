Site Introduction

Welcome to my little (work in progress) personal corner!

Some part of me has gotten super tired with social media/how online spheres feel, so I wanted to use my tumblr knowledge of CSS and HTML to make a site that's my own little hidey hole for myself, and whoever passes by.

This site is semi-optimized for mobile, but it's not perfect, and it does make heavy use of javascript (though it'll probably look ugly already if it's disabled for yall). I do update it pretty frequently since it's my fixation at the moment and I have many pages I still plan to make.

Although this site is SFW, I'll discuss mature topics and tend to like making more crude jokes (pp jokes funny sorry). As this will mostly be a personal site, you can expect these things:

  • OC corners for my main blorbos / shrines
  • Cool resources I like and have found over the years
  • My favourite things / media I consume
  • A mini blogging / diary corner where I ramble
  • Game related things or recipes I like
  • Yume / OC x Canon stuff cause I'm stupid pilled over them

Slowly making my dent in working on this over time ... god forbid if tumblr dies I guess I'll settle here.
