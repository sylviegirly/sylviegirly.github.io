Welcome to my little (work in progress) personal corner! Sorry, you'll find weird WIP sections in my home page cause it's easier to test here.

This site is semi-optimized for mobile, but it's not perfect. I do update it pretty frequently since it's my fixation at the moment and I have many pages I still plan to make. Although this site is SFW, I'll discuss mature topics and tend to like making more crude jokes (pp jokes funny sorry). As this will mostly be a personal site, you can expect these things:

  • A mini blogging / diary corner where I ramble
