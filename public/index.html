Welcome to my little personal corner!

Some part of me has gotten super tired with social media/how online spheres feel, so I wanted to use my tumblr knowledge of CSS and HTML to make a site that's my own little hidey hole for myself, and whoever passes by. This site is relatively optimized for mobile, but it's not perfect, and it does make heavy use of javascript (though it'll probably look ugly already if it's disabled for yall).

Although this site is SFW for the most part, I'll discuss mature topics, swear, and may rarely talk about more NSFW stuff which will be appropriately tagged/labeled. As this will mostly be a personal site, you can expect these things:

  • OC corners for my main blorbos / shrines
  • My artwork over the years
  • Cool resources I like and have found over the years
  • My favourite things / media I consume
  • Game related things or recipes I like
  • Yume / OC x Canon stuff cause I'm stupid pilled over them
  • Frequent updates since I work on this site daily pretty much

Slowly making my dent in working on this over time ... god forbid if tumblr dies I guess I'll settle here.
