Minecraft Forever World

All images are from newest to oldest, so you can see my forever world budding from uh ... when I picked the game up again! My world was originally made in December 2023, but I only began playing more earnestly in March 2025.

Minecraft has always been a little scary for me as a game concept, if only due to the fact that sandbox games have too many options and I freeze up! But I think I've found myself a little forever home and world I've been enjoying exploring, and want to document it more thoroughly rather than just have snippets I've posted on my tumblr, or in discord chats. I am very late to the minecraft party, but I'm excited to share it here.

My general game 'manifesto' is:

  • I will keep my builds small, big builds scary to me and I have no patience
  • I want to connect all my villages / cool structures to my base with decorated paths
  • If I make farms, they will be minimal / aesthetic focused
  • Creative mode is ok for builds!! I do not wanna chop 100 trees or burn out on a project!!
    • If creative mode is used, I will not add blocks/creatures I do not have access to/have not found (ex. I will not use acacia wood since I've not found it yet)

Older Update Logs / Diary
11/25/25

Finished making a little copper golem system for organizing, but it's on a very small scale. I'm sure I'll also outgrow it in due time, but it's doing what it needs to for now in dropping off crap I don't need at the moment.

I really should start pondering making mob/XP grinders ... problem is so many are so ugly and there aren't many aesthetic ones that don't make me wanna explode into viscera to build or need me to make a scaffold up into the asscrack of the world to wait out spawns. This one is tempting but it needs armadillos which ... I haven't found. I do use creative mode in some instances but I do not want to put in items of stuff I haven't genuinely found yet in the world.

I did make a farm area to host a coop of chickens and a trio of pigs, sheep, and cows. I'd still wanna make some farms that insta spit out meat + leather + raw chicken but that's later. Somehow the chickens escaped ... do they need 2 block high fences...? I could lower the ground though but I was lazy.

I also caved to finally install some mods, though it's mostly the Fabulously Optimized mod pack, a pretty shader pack, and better leaves. I don't wanna add any gameplay changing mods, I just like seeing pretty leaves and colours, though I'm trying very hard to not get distracted by all the shiny visual mods.

11/24/25

Err ... well it's been a good long while since I last played ... honestly I didn't take any screenshots, but I'm making a new base and want to finish it first; it's not far from my old one at all, and a nether portal connects the two, but I really wanted to make a new house close to the village I revamped. I want a chest system that's more organized, dedicated to just certain piles, or woods, or stone types (to put it less eloquently, I want a space to have a buttfuck amount of chests).
