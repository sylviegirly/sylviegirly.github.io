Story

SPOILERS CAN AND WILL BE PRESENT up until the end of Stormblood, please be aware of that

Intro

Hailing from the Azim Steppe himself under the Khatayin tribe, Khaliun has gotten fed up with the cycling, unchanging lifestyle of the nomadic goat hunters, of the Naadams he never partook in, and the feuding of the tribes. Seen as a brother and warrior to his clan, having his own Yol, it did hurt to eventually leave the clan around the age of 16-18 to find something greater in his life, even amidst the Garlean incursion in Doma.

A tribe which largely remains unseen, hunting goats in the mountains for nine moons of the year. The remaining three are spent at the foot of the great north range, where they survive off the dried meat they stocked.
