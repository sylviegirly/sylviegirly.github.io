About

When standing on all four limbs with its neck in a normal position, its height reaches to about 7’1” and about 13’ in length from snout to tail. Soft and short fur covers their body, while it has a mane that begins at the top of their head and falls down to their shoulders. The fur at the tips of their paws seems to ‘harden’ into claws, and can easily be used for climbing or when fighting. Teeth line the inside of their mouth, though they are not visible, and ithas a long tongue that can be useful when cleaning himself or reaching objects. It is an omnivore, though leans towards meat and sweets. Their emotions are easily visible in their tail movements and body language. It has a harder time speaking in this form, having to struggle to make out works—their native language comes out in yips, chirps and purrs.
