Sylvie is the vessel of the God of Trees—a Dendro elemental lifeform adopted as a god by the people of Sumeru—that went by the name of Foras. Disillusioned with the aspect of godhood upon their revival from a death suffered during the Archon War, they have integrated into human life and have been travelling all over Teyvat for the last 1,500 years.

Many would call them loud in different ways—in voice, energy, and their personality in general; one would not think they were anything but an adventurer that was way, way too enthusiastic and reckless for their own good. The years Sylvie has been alive have put them through a number of events and situations that have shaped their view of their world.

Details

demeanor
at their core, sylvie is a level headed person beyond the silly and humorous attitude they often tend to default to. they are a loving person at heart and tend to be warm to most people they meet, if not a little childish and silly if they come to like you. true in their convinctions, with an unwavering personality.
temperament
confident, sassy, and reassured of themselves; it's hard to not hear sylvie before you see them. they're loud and full of life and energy, finding enjoyment in the smallest of things. it's hard to ever find them without a grin or smirk on their face and a glimmer in their eyes.
temperament
an extrovert at their core, it's rare to find them by their lonesome; often sylvie can be located talking to people. conversations flow easily, and if their companions are more withdrawn, they feel no pressure in maintaning and continuing conversations on their own.
intellect
due to sylvie's long-lived lifespan and the many, many experiences they've seen and lived through, they've grown to be observant of people and habits. although their own knowledge runs deep, their intellect is best shown in reading people and reacting on the spot to situations ... strategizing is not their strong suit, and they tend to prefer thinking and doing in the moment, which has lead to a metaphorical foot in the mouth.
strengths
friendly, open, good-natured, and warm, sylvie is someone who will stick by your side through thick and thin with a stupid smile on their face. they are level-headed and tend to rely on humour to help ease stressful situations while thinking on their feet how to resolve things, or open to offering a listening ear. fun-loving and sees the whimsy and joy in living despite their age.
flaws
stubborn but in a way that's annoying, sylvie tends to be the type to dig their heels in when they believe their own ideas and truth; although open to listening to others, it is hard to get them to sway. they do not open up easily to people, despite their very chipper and open demeanour, more often people will realize they know very little about them in the grand scheme of things. nosy, rash, and often acts without thinking, which has lead them into situations where they've done more harm than good, or gotten hurt in the process.
ambitions
with a 500 year absence from being in the dark sea, sylvie is curious how the world has changed in that time; they are a traveller still at heart, and enjoy sponging in the experiences of the world to digest and indulge in, even if they are never satiated truly from their wanderlust. with the traveller's arrival and how things are slowly and quietly stirring, their curiosity is driven to see and follow alongside each beat of the story.
notes
  • it is rare for sylvie to discuss much with others when it comes to their own insecurity; after years of being a god-like figure, it's been integrated into their brain that 'gods' do not show weakness. only zhongli and a few others have seen their more vulnerable side.
  • terribly competitive with the right people or circumstances; do not play TCG against them, they are someone who is not shameless to make absolutely disgusting decks to fight against.
  • sylvie, although acting foolish and silly, is quick-witted and able to analyze situations easily; this simple, welcoming personality is not a ruse as they are truthful with it, but it does help to disarm people.
  • ... sometimes they truly are stupid though and do say some dumb, unfiltered stuff.

Story

Section Title

Space is vast, full of endless possibilities, the ancient one whose eyes have seen the worlds beyond him, and all around him, full of precious life, knowledge, people, more ... he goes on to the next peak of his endless interests. There is so much to see, even if he knows it all, and the possibilities they have laid before themselves. Eyes that reach vastly across all time and space. The toothy grin of perhaps pride grows on him. It was always so fascinating to see change and how species grow.

The worlds he has visited were always short visits, nothing ever beyond a few months since he's his curiosity was generally filled within the time frame, he couldn't say it was especially boring or unfilling, but he never really had any reason to stay to them, almost all of everything was the life that had a purpose that kept them moving in a rhythmic existence, and you cannot completely wish to watch more of it.

But the planet Earth caught his interest, more than others since living here, the humans, had odd movements of life, they acted in harmony, others times pure chaos. His toothy grinned returned. This was interesting to him, how they reacted to him was also new. He mimicked their humanness for a little while, getting used to the human language as a whole.

Design Notes

  • Their eyebrows are green, I just tend to default to drawing them black; artists can do either or!
  • Their shirt opening can be simplified to not have any strings.
  • Their pupils act like a cat's, being able to dilate or turn into slits; by default I tend to draw them slitted.
  • They have sharp, canine-like teeth.
  • Their vision is not a necessity to draw if need be.
  • The fern they wear is only on the left side of their head.
  • Major pectoralis is the body reference they're based off.
  • Their full design sheet is here on Toyhouse, including additional design notes + 3D turnarounds.

Trivia

  • Sylvie cannot handle spice to save their life, despite a deep love of spicy food in general; they faked their spicy intolerance so hard it became real.
  • Their ears are quite sensitive to touch, it may cause them to protectively cover their ears if anyone touches them or breathes close to them.
  • They can eat everything for the most part because their body can digest practically anything (minus anything originating from the void/abyss realm). even so, they are a massive foodie and prefer actual food.
  • They tend to view grooming (brushing one's hair, etc) as a bonding, affectionate activity, so friends may find Sylvie working on their hair to fix it (or licking it if they're in their deer form).
  • Sylvie has no true human body; they have no organs inside of them and they fake their blood being 'red', in reality, they are just a human bag wrapped around pure dendro essence (thus their true 'blood' is green).
