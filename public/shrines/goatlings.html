Hello Motherfuckers

Come one, come all! Welcome to the Kingdom of Goatlings!

Welcome to my little Goatlings Shrine, made as the end of service for Goatlings has basically arrived ... I'm um ... I'm still working on this, I kinda speedran finishing the bones to post it as a template so I kinda left my portion of the shrine half cooked LOL. I joined Goatlings in 2017 and have been playing on and off for the last 8 years, and I had the cool, cool ID of #49999. Literally one off of #50000 ...

I really enjoyed the simplicity of it, the gorgeous art styles, the ability to submit art that could become part of the site, the close-knit community, and the way the moderators and staff felt like a part of the community. This is kind of just an ode to the site, and a remake in its style (but not using tables, lol. sorry if there's one thing I hated was relying on tables + stripped CSS to do anything for profile layouts).

(Only reason the header is 'Hello Motherfuckers' is because I had it as a placeholder and someone thought it was funny so I'm keeping it.)
