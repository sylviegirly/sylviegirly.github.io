Minecraft Forever World

All images are from newest to oldest, so you can see my forever world budding from uh ... when I picked the game up again! My world was originally made in December 2023, but I only began playing more earnestly in March 2025.

Minecraft has always been a little scary for me as a game concept, if only due to the fact that sandbox games have too many options and I freeze up! But I think I've found myself a little forever home and world I've been enjoying exploring, and want to document it more thoroughly rather than just have snippets I've posted on my tumblr, or in discord chats. I am very late to the minecraft party, but I'm excited to share it here.

My general game 'manifesto' is:

  • I will keep my builds small, big builds scary to me and I have no patience
  • I want to connect all my villages / cool structures to my base with decorated paths
  • If I make farms, they will be minimal / aesthetic focused
  • I will make a nether highway at some point
