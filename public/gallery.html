Aid's Art Gallery

A simple page to slowly, slowly host my artwork in a singular place that isn't all across my social media (or err ... nuked off my DeviantART). Of course, all my art social medias are linked here! Sketches will usually not be included unless I really like them or feel like I wanna show them off (I usually only ever post those on my personal tumblr or the character's associated toyhouse). NSFW artwork will not be present here.

Art for the most part will not be organized by when it was made in the year, though newer art I will add after the creation of the page will be at the front. I will try my best to have alt text for everything, but some might be a lot less detailed.

I draw using a 6th gen 13-inch iPad Pro and in Clip Studio Paint (though I still have my Wacom Pro, it's just been retired for the time being).
