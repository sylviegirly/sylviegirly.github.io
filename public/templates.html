Templates

I am very new to making templates for websites, mostly since ideas for layouts and such don't come very easily to me (or I feel others may have already done something similar). As such it'll be very rare for me to make them, but I will host them here!! My templates tend err on simpler, and mobile friendly.

The credits do link to my VGen (mostly since I want to limit how much stuff goes back my personal sites) but you're free to edit the credit to link to here instead, or to remove the credit if you want (just makes it easier for people to come here!).

You can download them off Github, or off VGen/ko-fi only if you'd like to tip; all my templates will always be free and accessible on Github as well.

I also track templates others have made in my bookmarks page.
